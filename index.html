Cappuccino

Cappuccino is an Italian coffee drink made from espresso, hot milk and steamed condensed milk froth. The name derives from the Capuchin friars, which are related to the color of their habits.

Espresso

Espresso is a coffee preparation method of Italian origin, in which a small amount of near-boiling water is forced through finely ground coffee beans under 9-10 bar pressure.

Rs.150

Americano

Caffè Americano is a type of coffee drink prepared by diluting an espresso with hot water, which gives it a different flavor than traditionally brewed coffee.

Latte

Caffe Mocha

A caffè mocha, also known as a moccasino, is a chocolate-flavored hot drink that is a variant of a caffè latte, usually served in a glass rather than a mug.
